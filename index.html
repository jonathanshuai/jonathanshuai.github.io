Capstone Project 2: Recipes From Food Images

2018

In this project, I use PyTorch and transfer learning to train a convolutional neural network to recognize and identify food items in images. The model recognizes 52 food items with over 92% accuracy on the validation set. Using Angular and Flask, I made a simple web application to give recipe suggestions based off images of ingredients. We often have leftover ingredients that are thrown out because we don't know what to do with them. I envision this kind of technology helping with food waste issues, as well as being used in smart fridges or as a phone app.

Flight Delay Predictions

2017

I worked with a partner to analyze flight delays as a project final for a data science course in college. We used United States flight delay information from 2015 for this project, examining trends and looking at delays for 14 airlines and 322 airports. We then implemented a predictive model to predict whether a flight would be delayed by over 15 minutes with a ~79% accuracy without any information about the weather.
