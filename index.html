Flight Delay Predictions

2017

I worked with a partner to analyze flight delays as a project final for a data science course in college. We used United States flight delay information from 2015 for this project, examining trends and looking at delays for 14 airlines and 322 airports. We then implemented a predictive model to predict whether a flight would be delayed by over 15 minutes with a ~79% accuracy without any information about the weather.
